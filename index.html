Akan Name Generator

This is a web applicaton that takes in your birth date and computes the day of the week you were born then displays your Akan name depending on your gender as well.

Akan names are a West African naming system based mainly in Ghana and Togo that indicates what to name a child based on the day of the week they were born and their gender.

To find out your Akan name, enter your details in the form below then click on generate.
